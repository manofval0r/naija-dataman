I am Philip Okposo , a data analytics trainer, learning management specialist, and adjunct lecturer driven by a singular mission: making data work for everyone. With an MSc in Data Science and a BSc in Computing, I blend rigorous academic foundations with hands-on expertise gained from training leading organizations and shaping the next generation of IT professionals.
